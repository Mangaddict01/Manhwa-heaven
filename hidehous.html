Description

Park Gyu, a 'destructionist' who predicted that the world would end three years later and prepared for it in every way. And as he predicted, one day, the whole world enters a nuclear war starting with the nuclear bombing of Seoul. An unprecedented apocalypse unfolds. Despite everyone's ridicule, the main character Park Gyu, who has been quietly digging his own bunker for three years, emptying all his assets, finds it worth it. Was it just a coincidence that he foresaw all of this?
