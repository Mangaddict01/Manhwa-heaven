Description

A world where people's memories are manipulated so that they cannot feel misfortune. A boy lives in that world as a 'memory manipulator'. One day, when he becomes accustomed to life in the manipulated world, he realizes that innocent people are being sacrificed because of the rules set by the world. Will he protect the manipulated peace or save the people? The boy now begins to confront the world.
